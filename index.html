ICMR Healthcare: gene analysis for cancer detection

The project aims to address the rising concerns about various cancers by identifying the genes responsible for each cancer type, ultimately enabling early detection and reducing fatality rates.

Youtube Video Data from Most Popular Fitness Channels featuring women

Physical health is a huge component of overall well being. Personally, I exercise mostly by following YouTube videos created by female fitness influencers. As someone who is grateful to these people for sharing such valuable content for free, I wish to understand what does it take to succeed in this space, and what kind of fitness content is consumed by the rest of the world.
I have used the YouTube API to get data for 10 of the most popular Youtubers in the fitness space and conducted exploratory data analysis to see if I could discover some interesting patterns and insights.
I have included suggestions for aspiring creators in the TLDR; section of the README file, but to quote a few:

  • Create 5-15 minute videos (warm ups, stretches, ab workouts, etc.) for repeat viewership and to help those who either strapped for time or want to start/maintain their exercise streak.
  • Include terms like "Fat burn" , "hiit" (high intensity interval training), "challenge" in your titles : they are quite popular - catering to those who have fat/weight loss as their target.
  • Being descriptive doesn't hurt: mentioning the parts of the body that are being targeted - leg , booty, thigh. Don't be afraid to write longer titles and use plenty of tags (20-30).
